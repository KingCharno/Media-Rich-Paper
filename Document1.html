INTRO

League of Legends is one of the most popular competitive game with a very popular cometitive scene, players worldwide, a Netflix orginal series, now counting 5 successful spin-off games-you get it; Riot Games has been very successful with League of Legends. A game where a team of 5 are pitted against a team of five each playing different characters with their respective playstyles, moves, classes, and narrative. But how do you tell a story in a game that is only interested in being a competative multiplayer game? Riot has answered with... you don't. Allow me to explain; The most you will get out of League of Legends is a one sided interaction from your own characters perspective on another character that they encounter. Riot has religated their story telling to outside media that explains the origins, characteristiscs, and actions of a character and then have the gameplay reflect that narrative. An example I want to discuss is Viego: The Ruined King.

THESIS STATEMENT

I want to discuss Viego's narrative, visuals, and gameplay and how he subverts expectations almost consistently.
